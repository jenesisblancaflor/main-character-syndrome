What are some of the treatments for main character syndrome?

If you are suffering with main character syndrome, here are some treatments to stop the mitigate and stop the further spread of the syndrome.

Awareness of the Difference Between the Online Space and Reality

The first step in treating MCS is obtaining a reality and relearning the difference between the real world and the online world. Often times, people with MCS need to be shown that the numbers that they see online do not have much merit and not everything's worth should be quantified by the numbers they see online.

Destruction of the Influencer

Decrease In Consumer Capitalism

In a world where consumption is constant, people often times find them either indulging in expensive items to show off, or shilling products to their audiences. This can range from crytocurrency to makeup palletes. Once someone with MCS realizes material item aren't everything and there is more worth in the world other than bitcoin and Shein, they are finally free of the consumer mindset.

Get Off Your Phone!!

This treatment online is often refered to as "touching grasss." This implies those affected should get of their phone and go outside. This has been proven with a 100% success rate to be benefical in curing main chacacter syndrome as people simply can't be the main character on the internet.
