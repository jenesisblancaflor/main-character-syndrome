What is Main Character Syndrome?


Main character syndrome, also known as MSC, is a syndrome quickly plauging America. According to Psycology Today MCS, “Refers to a wide range of behaviours and thoughts, but, at root, it is when somebody presents, or imagines, themself as the lead in a sort of fictional version of their life (usually their own, although sometimes, disturbingly, somebody else’s), and presents that "life" through social media.” Basically, these people play chacacters online for some ulterior motive. If you think you or a loved one may be suffering from this syndrome, read more about it below.
